Always Betray

The exact oposite of Always Cooperate, in this strategy the player always chooses to defect.

Sometimes referred to as the "hawk" strategy.

This strategy does extremely well if it encounters players who choose to cooperate. When meeting other "hawk" players, this strategy performs rather poorly. If the other player decides to betray, both players get the penalty.

In fact, the results of a game between 2 "always betray" players is used as the lower baseline when analyzing the effectiveness of other strategies.

  • A bad guy in a town of good people will exploit them all to death.
  • Bad guys in a town full of bad guys will fight each other without getting any benefits.
